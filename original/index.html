BLANK PAGE STUDIO is a space for people dedicated to the advancement of innovative practices and ideas in art, architecture, design, and culture. Our mandate is to engage a broad audience throughout Calgary’s communities in generating dialogue about, and active participation in the city’s cultural sphere.


Designed as a hub for local emerging creative professionals, the studio was founded in 2013 by visual artist daniel j kirk, co-created by graduate architects Ivan Ostapenko and Studio North, and also includes Market Collective and Intelligent Futures.


We welcome collaborations with groups and individuals working to improve communities through thoughtful integration of art and design into the future of the city’s development.

We provide event space for various community needs, including: presentations, workshops, fundraising, group meetings, etc.

Our space is equipped with all the furnishings typically required for social and educational functions, including a kitchenette and projection screen. We also welcome proposals from artists and designers working in any discipline and medium, who wish to temporarily display their work in a semi-public setting. Are you looking for a temporary space at an affordable rate? Contact us now to discuss all the details.
